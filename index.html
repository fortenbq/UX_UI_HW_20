"Your Tutor Finder"

Alden

UX/UI Case Study

Project Overview

2 other UI / UX designers I were tasked with brainstorming on a relevant problem that could be solved using design thinking. After deliberating, we hypothesized that students who can build a relationship with an accessible tutor would have (more) academic success as opposed to other relevant options.

The Design Process

Research

We wanted to understand the user’s pain points and gains. In order to do this, we gathered 5 individuals who closely resembled our proto-persona (ideal user) and conducting interviews to gain more insight into the user’s perspective.

User Insights

Users prefer to study alone rather than groups

Users are open to tutors but they have a hard time finding the right tutor
